Burton Latimer Baptist Church

a community of faith, worship, and hope!

We are a welcoming Christian community nestled in the heart of Northamptonshire. In addition to our regular services, we have parent and toddler groups, youth club, services, social groups, table tennis, meals, and loads more. We would like to extend an open invitation to anyone who would like to join us. Keep an eye out here and on our Facebook page for events and activities..
